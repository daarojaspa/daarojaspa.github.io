Hi there, my name is Daniel Rojas and here is what I have been doing:

Fundamentals

Fundamentals is a blog where I break down complex tech and data science topics in simple terms — especially for non-technical readers. I cover subjects like how money works, how the internet works, and how natural language processing (NLP) works, aiming to make the fundamentals accessible to everyone.

The blog began in 2023, when I started writing on Medium about the topics I was learning at the time — front-end development, accessibility, game theory, and psychology — as a way to better understand myself. However, I soon realized that Medium is not a very accessible platform for screen reader users.

That motivated me to create my own blog, one that fully complies with WCAG 2.1 accessibility standards.

Fruit classifier Prototype for food banks

Fruit Classification CNN for Food Bank Automation (Prototype in development — 3 weeks to delivery) Developing a convolutional neural network (CNN) to classify fruit images by type and ripeness, aimed at automating sorting processes in food banks. This prototype supports resource optimization and reduction of food waste by improving the efficiency of donated food distribution.

NLP classifier

As part of a course on Platzi, I enhanced a text classification template by improving its structure and applying advanced text preprocessing techniques using NLTK for tokenization. I explored different cleaning strategies and classification algorithms to improve model performance. Throughout the project, I maintained clear and updated documentation in the README file, while learning to implement tracking, orchestration, and monitoring using tools such as MLflow, Prefect, and Grafana. I also leveraged my knowledge of Docker and Git to ensure reproducibility and version control. The final stage of the project is deploying the solution on a cloud platform — I’m currently working on that..

About Me

Data Scientist and Machine Learning Engineer with a Bachelor's in Mechatronics and a recent certification in data science from Platzi. Skilled in Pandas, scikit-learn, Prefect, Docker, FastAPI, and SQL, with hands-on experience building AI prototypes such as a CNN for fruit classification aimed at optimizing food bank sorting, and an NLP ticket classifier for bank complaints using NLTK, Prefect, and MLflow. Motivated by the vision of applying AI for social good, I seek opportunities that combine financial growth, intellectual challenge, and interdisciplinary problem-solving to create meaningful impact.
