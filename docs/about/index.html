NLP · MLOps

Bank-complaint ticket classifier

An end-to-end NLP pipeline that reads raw customer-complaint tickets and routes each one to the right category — removing manual triage. I built the preprocessing and tokenization with NLTK, compared cleaning strategies and classification algorithms, and wrapped the whole thing in a reproducible MLOps stack: experiment tracking in MLflow, orchestration with Prefect, monitoring in Grafana, and Docker for reproducibility. Computer Vision · Social Impact

Fruit-ripeness classifier for food banks

A convolutional neural network that classifies donated fruit by type and ripeness from images, so food banks can sort and distribute donations before they spoil. The goal is practical: turn a slow manual sorting step into an automated one and cut food waste in the donation chain. Built in PyTorch as a working prototype.
